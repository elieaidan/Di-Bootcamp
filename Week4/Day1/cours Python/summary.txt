[Math operators]

    + addition
    - substraction
    * multiplication
    / division
    // floor (return the closest number on the left [5//2 => 2])
    % modulo (return the remainer of the division)

    ** power (5 ** 5 => 25)


[conditional operators]

    < less than
    <= less or equal

    > greater than
    >= greater or equal

    == equal 
    != not equal


[Value types]

Numerical
    Integer (<class 'int'>) - whole number (4)
    Float (<class 'float'>) - floating number (4.4)

Strings (<class 'str'>) - 
    1. piece of text 
        '$@#@# ijdfjew 3412334'
    2. sequence of strings
        'hello'[3] -> 'l'
    
Boolean (<class 'bool'>) 
    True
    False
